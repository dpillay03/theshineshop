    WHO ARE WE?

    Established in 2005, the Shine Shop is an automotive detailing business serving the greater Seattle area and beyond. With over a decade of experience, we have detailed personal vehicles, commercial vehicles, fleet vehicles for law enforcement and firefighting agencies, RV's and Semi Trucks, boats and everything in between.
